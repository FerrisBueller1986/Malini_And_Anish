2016

I sent an email, she read it, she replied, I replied, we had our first date way back on the 25 July 2016. She did crossfit, we ran a lot, we became herbivores, we met the families, I lost a white brother but gained a black sister.

We had no doubts

br

2017

I'm O Positive, she's Merlot Positive, we made pour decisions, we ate a lot, we ran some more and we took our first trip over the seas to Georgia.

Stronger Together

br

2018

We went to Spain, we made some friends, we partied in Ibiza, we drank some wine, we watched some shows and we forged an unshakeable bond.

Teamwork

br

2019

We ran our first marathon, we rescued a doggy, I went more grey, she became more beautiful, we did everything together.

The planning begins

br

2020

We asked for almond milk, we laughed, we video called, I grew a beard, so did she, we learned the value of each others company when we were 1635km apart but we were there for each other.

She said yes!

We said farewell to our loved ones, we cried. We hiked up a mountain, she was surprised. She likes carrots, I put it on a ring, she said yes, we bought a house.
